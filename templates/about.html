About Bhartiya Boudh Mahasabha

The Bhartiya Boudh Mahasabha is committed to promoting the teachings of Lord Buddha and the values of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. We work toward social transformation through Dhamma, organizing awareness campaigns, spiritual teachings, and social development initiatives that spread harmony, equality, and brotherhood in society.

Our Mission

To uplift society through the Dhamma teachings of non-violence, compassion, and wisdom. We aim to empower every individual through knowledge, equality, and unity.

Our Vision

To build a peaceful and awakened society where every individual lives with dignity, guided by the noble path of Buddhism and Ambedkarite thoughts.
